Our Story

A table shaped
by memory

Saffron & Stone was born from a simple conviction: that the best meal you'll ever have tastes like somewhere you belong. Our kitchen draws on India's layered culinary heritage — slow-cooked techniques, hand-ground masalas, ingredients sourced from farms we know by name — and frames them in a setting designed for long evenings and unhurried conversation.

Every dish is a considered act. We change our menu with the seasons, not the trends.

Behind the Kitchen

Chef Arjun Mehta

Arjun trained under three Michelin-starred kitchens in Paris and Lyon before returning to Mumbai with a single ambition: to cook Indian food on its own terms, without apology or pastiche. His philosophy is restraint — knowing which flavours to silence so the right one can speak.

"Spice is not heat. It is depth, and depth takes patience. We have time."
